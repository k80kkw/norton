Norton Add Corrie

After featuring in the last two pre-season friendlies and notching two assists, Courtney Corrie has put pen to paper and committed to Norton and Stockton Ancients FC Ladies for the 2017/2018 season.
Signing from Durham WFC, Courtney is a talented player who attracted interest from a number of other clubs during the close season. Assistant manager Kevin Waterworth commented on the most recent signing, "Courtney is player both Chris and myself have admired for a while; she is a player who is comfortable in a number of attacking positions, and will no doubt chip in with a number of goals and assists. We are delighted to have acquired another player with WSL experience as we look to build on last season and push for promotion to the WPL."
We would like to welcome Courtney to the club and wish her luck for the coming season.
