NORTON & STOCKTON ANCIENTS LADIES FC

NEXT SEASON.

Sunday 5th February 2017:

Norton and Stockton Ancients Ladies are planning for the future by putting in place a reserve team that will compete in the Durham FA development league next season. The aim is to create a clear pathway for the junior girls to progress into the womens game.

Along with that, the club will still have an U18s team who will compete in the Russell Foster League on a Saturday morning, which will again ensure that there are plenty of opportunities for girls in the area to progress at a local club with progression at the heart of everything.

With the U15s also moving up to the U16s, the future looks positive for years to come. The club will also have an U14s side, two U12s teams, an U10s team and a new U9s team for 2017/18.
